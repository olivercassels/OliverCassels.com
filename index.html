What I do

I'm a specialist executive search adviser focused exclusively on the global food and agribusiness sector. With a career spent at the intersection of food, agriculture and business, I work with boards and leadership teams to identify and appoint senior executives and non-executive directors who can genuinely move things forward.

The value chain I cover runs from farm inputs, primary production and agri-technology through to food manufacturing, ingredients, foodservice and investment. Most of my work is at C-suite, VP and board level, where the right appointment matters enormously and the margin for error is low.

I work with a small number of clients at any one time, by design. That means you'll have my direct attention throughout — not a junior associate who's never met your leadership team.

I am currently an Associate Managing Director at Kincannon & Reed, a leading global food and agribusiness search firm, through which client mandates are managed.

About me


I grew up on a farm in Wiltshire and have spent my career moving across the food and agribusiness world — in operations, technology, investment and now executive search. That breadth isn't accidental: it means I understand what leadership looks like from the inside, not just from the outside.

Before joining Kincannon & Reed, I worked in agricultural software and data platforms at Map of Agriculture and Origin Digital, built agricultural ventures in East Africa as a Business Development Manager in Kigali, and held a talent partner role at a venture-backed fintech. The thread running through all of it is genuine interest in the sector and the people who shape it.

My academic background includes an MSc in Agricultural Economics from the University of Reading, an MA in Political Science and Geography from Trinity College Dublin, and more recently a Cambridge Certificate in Sustainable Food Production and Processing. I'm a fellow of the New Entrepreneurs Foundation.

I'm based in Wiltshire, and I work globally.
